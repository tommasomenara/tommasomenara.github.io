Short Bio

I am a PhD student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at University of California Riverside under the supervision of Prof. Fabio Pasqualetti.
I completed a Laurea Magistrale degree (M.Sc. equivalent) in Robotics and Control Engineering at University of Pisa, Italy, in 2016, and a Laurea degree (B.Sc. equivalent) in Mechatronics Engineering at University of Padova, Italy, in 2013.
